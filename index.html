About Us

Dr. Himanshu Trivedi (MDS in Periodontology from Aligarh Muslim University) is a renowned dental practitioner with expertise in Periodontology, Implant Dentistry, Esthetic Dentistry, Oral Surgery, and more. With 26 publications, 19,125 reads, and 46 citations, Dr. Trivedi is actively involved in research on Chemical Biology, Bone Regeneration, and Bone Grafts, with a current project titled "Quackery in Dentistry: A Field Study!"
